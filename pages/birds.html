Cockatiel

The cockatiel is the only member of the genus Nymphicus. It was previously unclear whether the cockatiel is a crested parakeet or small cockatoo; however, more recent molecular studies have assigned it to its own subfamily, Nymphicinae.

Cockatiel Favorite Things in Order!

  1. They love fresh fruit
  2. A honey stick or millet spray once a month as a special treat.
  3. Cheek rubs
